# Veeko visual prompt set

All scenes use one consistent character anchor: a premium, cute 3D Shopify intelligence companion with a pale ice-aqua hood and cape, glossy black teardrop face, soft cyan eyes, champagne-metal headphones and fingers, rounded toy-like proportions, tactile fabric and polymer materials, calm confidence, clean pale-aqua studio lighting, no text, no logos, no watermark.

## Hero companion

Centered square character portrait. Veeko sits calmly with hands together, direct eye contact, generous pale-aqua negative space, soft cyan rim light, understated champagne-metal details, premium product-render finish.

## Risk analysis

Landscape editorial product scene. Veeko sits on the right, thoughtfully reviewing a translucent order list on the left. A luminous protective shield sits between Veeko and the data; one restrained coral risk signal is highlighted. Keep the interface abstract and text-free.

## AI assistant

Landscape conversational scene. Veeko sits on the left and gestures toward a sequence of floating translucent message shapes on the right. Use a soft lavender architectural alcove, clear negative space, and text-free interface forms.

## Analytics

Landscape analytics scene. Veeko stands on the right and presents a tactile glass bar chart, a small customer cluster, and a clean revenue arc on the left. Keep the composition calm, legible, dimensional, and free of numbers or text.

## Closing protection scene

Very wide 16:9 scene. Preserve large clean negative space across the left half. Place Veeko on the far right holding a small neutral commerce parcel inside a luminous protective arc, with subtle flowing signal paths crossing the floor.

## Motion treatment

Each scene is rendered as a seamless four-second VP8 loop at 16 fps using a restrained cosine ease-in/ease-out camera push, sub-pixel lateral drift, and a subtle light pulse. WebP posters use the same crop for reduced-motion and data-saver fallbacks.
